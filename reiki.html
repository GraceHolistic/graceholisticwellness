Reiki

What is Reiki?
Reiki is a complementary, healing energy, therapy. It is a centuries old Japanese technique that can help reduce stress as it induces deep relaxation. Once in this relaxed state the body’s parasympathetic nervous system begins to engage, this means that the body has switched into it’s ‘rest, digest, repair’ gear. Reiki is hugely beneficial for those living in the fast lane and feeling like they are trying to do it all and then a bit more. It is also a good addition to a regular self care regime as it assists with your response to the general stresses of life. Reiki is an immensly nurturing experience for the mind, body and soul.

What is healing energy?
Healing energy or life force energy, commonly referred to as chi or prana, flows through pathways in and around living things and it is this energy that gives us our ‘aliveness’ or vitality. The Reiki therapist assists in enabling this flow of energy.

Is Reiki religious?
Reiki is not religious but it is spiritual in nature. You do not have to have any particular beliefs to benefit from it – Reiki healing is available to anyone.

Is Reiki safe?
Yes, it is a non-invasive, very simple therapy.

What to expect recieving Reiki at Grace Holistc Wellness
A full treatment lasts for an hour. It begins with a connection ritual consisting of an informal consultation and a cleansing aromatherapy footbath. This is followed by a 45 minute healing session. The client lies comfortably, fully clothed on the massage table under warm blankets (a sitting session can also be arranged if preffered). Working intuitively with each individual the therapist places their hands gently around and on the client at key Reiki points.
